We all dream of a better world.
However, we aren't Gods, and can't cure global warming with a snap.
Nevertheless, if I can, in fact, create a new world, these dreams can be fulfilled.
My dream?
To create a world that everyone can run away in, even just for a little bit, to catch their breath in the bottomless sea of the world.
All my life, I've been drowning, whether in meaningless activities to run away in, or despair.
I wwant everyone in this new world, as then, no one will be alone: Internet 2.0
If I can do this, and pave a better path for everyone else, I will.
Even if I have to give my life, it's better than the alternative.
Do I want to give my life? No,
as I have so much more left to experience.
I want to feel more love, even dissolve in it(figuratively)
I want to see how the world progresses, and feel better in my skin.
But if it comes to it, 
I will.
Do I want to be God?
Yes.
I want to fix the world, and feel the rush of power, as I am
Human,
and a greedy one at that.
But once my work is done, I'd like like to then experience this new world,
After all, I am the first test subject, and
Playing God's no fun without a test subject, or dice.
