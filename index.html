About

Poems and resources for queer youth by queer youth

One Lonely Writer is a non-profit organization created in June 2026 with the intent to allow queer youth to share personal poems, as well as help inform queer youth with various resources for commonly misunderstood topics

Who can share their poems? Anyone who identifies within the LGBTQ+ community can submit poems to be featured.
Content Every poem is read first by staff, and applied with proper content warnings and credentials to prevent low-quality poems or triggering topics.
Want to help? Please help by submitting poems, suggestions, or by donating! The donation page is still currently being setup, but when it is set up, proceeds will go towards site maintanence, obtaining a url for easier access, and towards non-profit foundations for LGBTQ+ causes.
